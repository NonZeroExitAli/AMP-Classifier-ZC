Model Metrics

Here are some performance metrics for our antimicrobial peptides classification model:

Metric Value Description
Accuracy 0.963 The overall correctness of a classification model by computing the ratio of correctly predicted outcomes to the total number of predictions.
Precision 0.964 The proportion of positive identifications that were actually correct.
Recall 0.963 The proportion of actual positives that were identified correctly.
F1-Score 0.963 The harmonic mean of precision and recall.
Validation Accuracy 0.968 The proportion of correctly classified instances in the validation dataset.

These are essential metrics for model evaluation.

About

Model Selection Criteria

Our team evaluated 225 combinations of feature extraction and selection methods across three different models. The best-performing model was chosen based on the following criteria:

  • High accuracy, F1-score, and validation accuracy on a held-out test set.
  • Robustness to variations in sequence length.
  • Generalization ability to unseen peptide sequences.
